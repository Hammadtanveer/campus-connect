Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

CampusConnect ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you create an account or use our app:

  • Account Information: Display name and email address
  • Profile Information: Profile picture, bio, and academic details you choose to provide
  • User Content: Notes, posts, and files you upload to the app
  • Usage Data: App interactions collected via Firebase Analytics
  • Device Information: Device type and OS version for crash reporting

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the CampusConnect service
  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • To display your profile and content to other users
  • To send password reset emails when requested
  • To improve app performance via crash reports and analytics
  • To send push notifications about campus events (with your permission)

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Firebase Firestore — for user profiles and app content
  • Firebase Storage / Cloudinary — for uploaded files and images
  • Firebase Authentication — for account credentials

All data is stored on secure servers with industry-standard encryption.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only with:

  • Firebase/Google — for app infrastructure (governed by Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Cloudinary — for media storage (governed by Cloudinary's Privacy Policy)
  • Law enforcement — only when legally required

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data at any time through the app
  • Update or correct your profile information
  • Delete your account and all associated data via Settings → Delete My Account
  • Request data export by contacting us

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all personal data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

CampusConnect is intended for college students (18+). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Push Notifications

We may send push notifications about campus events and updates. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.
